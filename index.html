About

Mental health is essential for overall well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. Good mental health helps us cope with stress, make healthy choices, and maintain positive relationships.

However, mental health problems are common. They can affect anyone, regardless of age, gender, or background. It's important to prioritize mental health and seek help when needed.

Tips for Maintaining Mental Health

  • Stay physically active
  • Eat a balanced diet
  • Get enough sleep
  • Practice relaxation techniques
  • Connect with others
  • Seek professional help if needed

Resources

If you or someone you know is struggling with mental health issues, seek help from a trusted healthcare professional or contact a mental health hotline.
